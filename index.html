Touhou Project

Touhou Project is a video game series that has made popular musical songs throughout the series. It started in 1997 by Jun'ya Ōta or his nickname ZUN. He made the game series due to his love of music and gaming.


Touhou Project music genre is are quite varied and different from each other due the style of music changes with each new game series released. The genre Touhou plays however are illusionary folk death piano medley orchestral viking jpop soundtrack acapela transe rock aphextwin techno instrumental midi eurobeat swing house set classical experimental hardcore traditional funkot latin relaxing DnB dubstep rege jazz metal.
